Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a character from the anime and manga series "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba."

He is known for his exceptional speed and agility, as well as his unique fighting style that incorporates thunder and lightning techniques.

Despite his cowardly and anxious personality, Zenitsu is a skilled swordsman and a valuable member of the Demon Slayer Corps.
